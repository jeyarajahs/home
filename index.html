What is Lorem Ipsum?

Unlike common belief, Lorem Ipsum is not randomly generated text majorly due to the fact that the usage of Latin within the text makes it difficult to comprehend. However a latin professor at the Hampden-Sydney College in Virginia looked into the matter further, by researching into one of the more odd words out within a Lorem Ipsum passage, the word was “consectetur” . After pursuing the research by reviewing other passages, it was evident that Lorem Ipsum originates from the sections 1.10.32 and 1.10.33 in "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (The Extremes of Good and Evil) by Cicero which was created in 45 BC, making Lorem Ipsum over 2000 years old!
